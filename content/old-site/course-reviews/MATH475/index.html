MATH475 Combinatorics and Graph Theory

What does it say on Testudo?

General enumeration methods, difference equations, generating functions. Elements of graph theory, matrix representations of graphs, applications of graph theory to transport networks, matching theory and graphical algorithms.

Why did I take this class?? Should I have taken this class???

Wiseley Wong was teaching it and the material is interesting. Honestly, I feel like I didn't learn that much though. Looking back, I probably shouldn't have taken it, but it also counts as a CS elective.

Review

Good class experience with Wiseley Wong. I've never felt so good at counting before. A lot of counting problems surprisingly can be solved by recursion (i.e. difference equations). The stuff about generating functions and Catalan numbers were the interesting parts for me.
The graph theory material was surprisingly boring to me since most of the material was stuff I had already seen before. The stuff about planar graphs and extremal graph theory was interesting though.
It feels like everyone is doing well in this class since the averages are insanely high.
